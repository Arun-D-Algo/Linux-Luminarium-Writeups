# FINAL NOTE

Completed Linux Luminarium on June 15, 2026.

This repository began as a collection of notes and slowly turned into a record of everything learned throughout the course. Along the way I explored Linux fundamentals, shell scripting, permissions, processes, users, environment variables, command-line tools, and security concepts through hands-on challenges.

Looking back at the first modules, even simple concepts such as pipes, variables, and redirection felt unfamiliar. By the end, challenges involved process inspection, command hijacking, symbolic links, environment variable leaks, filesystem destruction, and fork bombs.

This marks the completion of Linux Luminarium, but not the end of learning Linux. These writeups will remain here both as a reference for future revision and as a snapshot of one stage of my journey as a Computer Science student.
